About Us

As it is said, one blood donation can save 3 lives of people. Many a times, it happens that people who are in need of blood do not get blood at time due to which they have to fight to live. So, this application is made to make things easier for blood donation where we connect people to blood banks nearby them and help them get or donate blood.
